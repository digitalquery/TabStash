# Privacy Policy

**Tab Stash** does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What data is stored

All data (saved page URLs, titles, and timestamps) is stored locally in your browser using Firefox's built-in `storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device.

## What data is shared

Nothing. There are no analytics, no tracking, no remote servers, and no third-party services.

## Data export

You can export your saved items to a JSON file at any time via the sidebar panel. This file is saved to your local downloads folder.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer.
